Shan Nationalities Democratic Party (SNDP)

Shan State

Shan Nationalities Democratic Party (SNDP) was founded in 2010 by a breakaway group from the SNLD that disagreed with its boycott of elections that year. But it has struggled to gain a political base, and in the 2015 election won a single seat in the state and regional parliament. Its leader is Sai Aik Paung (Sai Aik Pao). He is a former central executive committee member of the SNLD who founded the SNDP to contest the 2010 election.
