Minh-Ha Le

A Graphic designer & Visual storyteller

Minh-Ha is a visual storyteller with expertise in editorial graphics, data visualizations, and animated explainers that elevate impactful journalism. She specializes in translating complex data and geographic trends into clear, accessible visuals for both digital and print media. Her work includes infographics, interactive maps, charts, and GIFs that support investigative reporting and enhance daily news coverage. Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Adobe Premiere), Figma, Flourish, Datawrapper, Power BI, and Mapbox, she collaborates closely with reporters, editors, and developers to create compelling visuals that maintain journalistic integrity and resonate with diverse audiences across platforms.
